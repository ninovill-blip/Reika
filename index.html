My Approach

Before I treat, I assess. I photograph your face from multiple angles under proper lighting. I examine facial proportions, skeletal structure, skin quality, and how your features move when you smile or speak. I only use FDA-approved products from authorized distributors, and I personally inspect every vial before use—checking lot numbers, clarity, seals, and storage temperatures.

During my days on stage and in front of cameras, I learned that faces tell stories in angles and shadows. Now, as a registered nurse, I combine that artist's eye with clinical precision. My consultation process is thorough because I won't compromise on safety or quality.
